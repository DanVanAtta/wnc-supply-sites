Uploading Needs

WNC-Supply-Sites.com is deeply integrated with R4 to track inventory needs and help dispatch.

The process starts when inventory is updated on WNC-Supply-Sites.com.

Below is a video of how the data flow looks, on the right are updates being made on WNC-Supply-Sites.com and the left is a visualization of the real time data flowing into R4.

Needs Dashboard

The uploaded inventory data lands in the R4 Needs-Dashboard where a dispatcher can see how many needs there are, and where.

Needs Matching

Clicking the Needs-Matching link takes a dispatcher to WNC-Supply-Sites needs matching table. The table shows which sites have needed supplies.

Drivers can Volunteer For Routes

Drivers can also volunteer for routes. By browsing through potential deliveries, drivers can click 'volunteer', enter their availability dates and then confirm. Dispatchers are notified of the volunteer opportunity and can then begin arranging the route.

Drivers Update Availability Through the Driver Portal

The driver portal allows drivers to update their availability. Dispatchers are then able to see which drivers are available.

Setting up a Delivery

After needs are matched, a dispatcher can then start setting up a delivery. This is done in the R4 system. The dispatcher creates the delivery, sets up the 'from' and 'to' sites, and adds items to the delivery.

Assigning a Driver

Now that we have a delivery set up, next we need to arrange transportation. To do this, we need to find and contact a driver. There are various options for finding drivers:

  • Drivers can volunteer for deliveries
  • Contact a driver directly from the driver rolodex
  • Connect with a driver in discord

Once done and a driver is aligned, we have a delivery set up!

Confirming the Delivery

Once the delivery is setup, we can send requests for confirmation from everyone involved. The dispatcher clicks "confirm", this sends SMS messages to the driver & pickup & drop off sites.

The driver, pickup and drop off sites are then sent SMS messages with a link to the delivery. They can each in turn click "confirm". If anyone clicks cancel, everyone will get a cancellation SMS message. Otherwise when all confirmations have come in, everyone will receive a "fully confirmed" SMS.

The dispatcher has a copy of the confirmation links. If needed, they can jump in and help the confirmations process.

Tracking the Delivery

Now that the delivery is fully confirmed, the driver gets a special link where they can start the actual delivery. They can first click to start the delivery and notify the pickup site they are on the way. They can then click again to confirm when they have arrived at the pickup site, when they are leaving, and when they have arrived at the final drop off. Each confirmation triggers SMS messages to the pickup sites notifying them the driver is on the way.
